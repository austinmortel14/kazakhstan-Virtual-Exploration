Leisure Activities

Kazakhstan offers a variety of activities for tourists:

  • Adventure & Nature: Hiking in the Tian Shan mountains, visiting the Charyn Canyon, and exploring the Kolsai and Kaindy Lakes.
  • Cultural & Historical Tours: Exploring Silk Road heritage sites such as Turkistan, Otrar, and Tamgaly petroglyphs.
  • Wildlife & Ecotourism: Visiting the Altyn-Emel National Park to see singing sand dunes and rare animals like the Przewalski's horse.
  • Urban Exploration: Discovering modern Almaty, visiting futuristic architecture in Astana (Nur-Sultan), and exploring Soviet-era monuments.
  • Extreme Sports: Skiing in Shymbulak, paragliding in the Almaty region, and desert off-roading in the Mangystau region.
